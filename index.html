Orango

ArangoDB Object Modeler for Node.js, Foxx and Web Browsers

Get Started →

Data modeler

Easily add structure to your data with models and schemas that can index, validate, pre-populate and query ArangoDB.

High level queries

Enjoy the dev experience of working with ArangoDB with an ORM backing your queries. Orango can create highly customizable queries from simple data stuctures.

Feature-rich

Orango provides validation, filtration, hydration, computed properties, indexing and more.
